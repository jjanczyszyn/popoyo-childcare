# Claude Spec: Popoyo Childcare Luxury In-Home Nanny Website

## Project Summary

Build a simple, luxurious website for **Popoyo Childcare**, an in-home nanny and vacation childcare service in the Popoyo area of Nicaragua.

The service sends vetted nannies to villas, hotels, Airbnbs, retreats, weddings, and family stays so parents can surf, attend spa appointments, go out to dinner, join retreat sessions, work, rest, or enjoy adult time while children are cared for nearby.

The site should feel premium, calm, trustworthy, warm, and extremely easy to book. This is not a cheap generic babysitter listing. It is a concierge-style family support service for tourists, expats, retreat leaders, villa managers, and visiting families.

Do not include implementation code in the output. Create the website according to this spec.

## Research-Based Pricing Rationale

Use USD pricing.

Current Nicaragua references suggest informal nanny or babysitter rates can be around **$3–$5/hour**, with higher rates for English-speaking or certified caregivers. However, those are local domestic-help rates, not tourist-facing, vetted, on-demand, villa-based childcare.

Comparable premium vacation childcare services in nearby travel markets charge much more:

- Costa Rica quality nanny/babysitting references commonly cite **$10–$15/hour**.
- Guanacaste vacation childcare services list **$15/hour**, a **4-hour minimum**, transport fees outside core zones, holiday surcharges, a 50% deposit, and a 72-hour cancellation policy.
- Los Sueños, Costa Rica babysitting service lists **$25/hour plus tax** for up to 4 kids, plus transportation.
- Mexico City hotel and holiday babysitting uses a **4-hour minimum**, child-count-based hourly rates, late-night fees, holiday double rates, and defined child-to-nanny ratios.
- Popoyo family resorts already arrange nanny services through external providers, which validates local demand for vacation childcare.

Therefore, Popoyo Childcare should price as a **premium mobile childcare concierge**, not as informal domestic help.

Pricing should be high enough to:

- Pay nannies fairly above local informal rates
- Cover travel time and transport
- Cover coordination and customer support
- Support vetting, training, and reliability
- Keep the brand premium
- Reduce low-quality, high-friction bookings

Yes. A 50% deposit is required to confirm your booking.

Cancellations more than 72 hours in advance can be refunded or transferred. Later cancellations may lose the deposit or incur the booking minimum.

## Accessibility Requirements

- Mobile-first layout
- Large readable text
- High contrast buttons
- Clear labels on all form fields
- No tiny gray text for safety information
- Form errors should be clear and kind
- Buttons should be easy to tap on mobile
- Language should be simple for non-native English speakers

## Launch Version Scope

### Must Have

- Luxury single-page website
- Services and pricing
- Trust & safety section
- Booking request form
- Price estimate
- WhatsApp booking request
- FAQ
- Service area
- Payment and cancellation policy
- Partner CTA

### Nice to Have

- Nanny profile cards
- Testimonials
- Partner logo section
- Instagram feed
- Recurring booking option
- Spanish version
- Admin dashboard
- Availability calendar

### Do Not Build in V1

- Instant payment
- Customer accounts
- Nanny marketplace
- Anonymous nanny booking
- Complex scheduling automation
- Live GPS tracking
- Medical intake beyond basic safety notes
- Any feature that implies guaranteed safety or replaces parental responsibility

## Success Criteria

The website is successful if:

1. A parent understands the service within 5 seconds.
2. Pricing feels premium but not absurd for Popoyo.
3. The booking flow works for multiple children.
4. Parents can request care without creating an account.
5. Safety boundaries are clear before booking.
6. The WhatsApp message includes all important details.
7. The site feels trustworthy enough for a parent to hand over child care.
8. Villa managers and retreat leaders understand how to partner.
9. The business can manually confirm bookings without operational chaos.
10. The service can later expand into spa + childcare packages with Popoyo Beauty.
